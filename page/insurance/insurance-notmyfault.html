The BBR Insurance Process

The Accident Was Not Your Fault.

You have three options.

Go through your own insurance - you will have to pay excess and allow your insurance company to claim that and any other losses back. Go through the insurance of the guilty party - this won’t affect your no claims bonus and you won’t have to pay your excess. Give the person who hit you the option to pay privately.
