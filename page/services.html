The Repair Process

So you bring your vehicle to BBR – what happens then?

1. You hand your vehicle to us and our friendly reception team will confirm your vehicle damage, price and your estimated completion date.

2. Your vehicle and job card will be handed over to our team of experienced technicians to begin repairs.

3. Your vehicle will be taken into the repair and metail shop who will complete all repairs and metal work.

4. Once the repairs and metal work is done you will be handed over to the paint shop where its prepared for primer and the vehicle is then premiered and prepared for paint.

5. Once the vehicle has been painted we will inspect and return to the strip and fit section where the ancillary parts are fitted back onto the vehicle.

6. The valeting section would be where the vehicle goes next to be polished, de-nibbed and valeted.

7. Lastly, the quality control check would take place before being handed back over to reception where we would call you and let you know the vehicle is ready to collect!
