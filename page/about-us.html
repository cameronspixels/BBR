About Us

Our Story

Established in 2004, BBR has had 13 years of providing high quality repair work for all makes and models of vehicles.

We were opened by locally qualified paint sprayer and panel beater Matt Greaves. He felt that 15 years of experience in the industry had given him the skills and ability to provide a quality, local car repair service.

Matt started out by himself, but now has a strong team of 9 staff including repairers, managerial staff and repairers.

The team at BBR firmly believe that each repair should be tailored to that customer’s individual needs, and they always strive to supersede expectations.

We also cover private insurance work, and all customers will be given use of a courtesy car while the repair takes place.
